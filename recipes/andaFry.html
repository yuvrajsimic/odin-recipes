Anda Fry Recipe

Description

Anda Fry is a spicy quick dish made by cooking boiled eggs then tossing it in indian spices mixture along with oil.

Egg Fry is an Indian side dish made by tossing hard boiled eggs in a spice mixture which includes turmeric powder, chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala powder, salt to taste and pepper powder.

Egg Fry shared here is one of the most requested egg dishes at home. This Egg Fry has become both kids favorite these days. My mother makes egg roast in a different way which calls for more ingredients but this Egg fry needs just eggs, oil, spices and can be made in dosa tawa itself.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cook eggs in boiling water for exactly 14 mins. Switch off add it to chill water, set aside for few mins. Then peel the shell of the eggs. Eggs are peeled and ready.
  2. Perfect hard boiled eggs are ready!
  3. To a pan or dosa tawa add 2 tablespoon oil. Heat it but should not be smoking hot. Keep flame in lowest once heated.
  4. .Give a quick mix – keep an eye so that you don’t burn the masalas.
  5. The oil masala mixture is ready.
